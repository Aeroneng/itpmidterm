Things to Do in Dumaguete City

Explore the vibrant city of Dumaguete and discover an array of exciting activities and attractions. From serene beaches and historic landmarks to adventurous outdoor experiences, there's something for everyone to enjoy in Dumaguete City.

Rizal Boulevard

The tranquil atmosphere and refreshing sea breeze make it an ideal spot for leisurely strolls and relaxation.

  • Famous seaside promenade located in Dumaguete City

Siliman University

Visitors can explore its lush campus and engage with students and faculty who are at the forefront of research and education.

  • Private research university located in Dumaguete City

Casaroro Falls

Getting to Casaroro Falls involves a thrilling trek through the dense jungle, across bamboo bridges, and along rocky paths.

  • A stunning waterfall located, near Dumaguete City
See Tours
Apo Island

Apo Island is renowned for its vibrant coral reefs and diverse marine ecosystem, making it a paradise for snorkelers and divers.

  • A small volcanic island
See Tours
Pulangbato Falls

Pulangbato Falls is characterized by its striking red rock formations, which contrast beautifully with the lush greenery surrounding the falls.

  • A scenic waterfall located, near Dumaguete City
See Tours
Twin Lakes Natural Park

Visitors can embark on nature walks or birdwatching excursions to spot colorful birds, rare orchids, and other unique wildlife thriving in their natural habitat.

  • A picturesque nature reserve located, near Dumaguete City
See Tours
Campanario de Dumaguete

Visitors can explore this well-preserved structure and learn about its role in the region's history, including its function as a watchtower to guard against pirate attacks.

  • A historic bell tower located in Dumaguete City

St. Catherine Of Alexandria Cathedral

Visitors can marvel at the cathedral's intricate details, including its towering spires, stained glass windows, and elegant arches.

  • A prominent Catholic church located in Dumaguete City

Tierra Alta Lighthouse

Visitors can soak in the stunning vistas and capture memorable photographs against the backdrop of this idyllic setting.

  • A tower structure that resembles a lighthouse
See Tours
Quezon Park

Visitors can explore the park's iconic monuments, including the statue of President Quezon, which commemorates his contributions to the nation.

  • A popular public park located in the heart of Dumaguete City
